Asset Allocation

We believe that asset allocation is the primary determinant of both long-term returns and the variance in those returns. We recommend strategic diversification and systematic portfolio rebalancing. We believe that taxable investors should limit tactical allocation manoeuvring. We respect the power of time to reduce volatility and to drive compound growth. Fin System uses risk factor exposures rather than asset classes as the core building blocks of portfolio construction.
